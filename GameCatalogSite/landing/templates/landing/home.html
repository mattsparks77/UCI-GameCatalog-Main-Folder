On Angel Island, a tribe of echidnas lives in harmony with the Chao. The Chao are peaceful creatures that protect the all-powerful Master Emerald, which balances the power of the seven mystical Chaos Emeralds. One of the echidnas, Tikal, befriends Chaos, an entity who protects the Chao. The power-hungry echidnas try to steal the power of the Master Emerald, massacring the Chao in the process. Horrified, Chaos harnesses the power of the Chaos Emeralds to transform into a monstrous beast, Perfect Chaos. Chaos destroys the island, but is stopped when Tikal seals herself and Chaos inside the Master Emerald.
